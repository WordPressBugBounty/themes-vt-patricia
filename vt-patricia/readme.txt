== Description ==
-------------------------------------------------------
VT Patricia is WordPress theme made for bloggers who wants to share your travel expertise, photography, fashion tips, food recipes, lifestyle, beauty and makeup, coaching, or any other niche blog. You can easily choose from 2 layout variation and color scheme to change your website look and feel with a single click. VT Patricia is translation-ready, WooCommerce compatible, footer logo support, custom widgets and many more. Check demo at https://volthemes.com/demo/?theme=vt-patricia
